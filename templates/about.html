About Plant Disease Detector

AI-powered agricultural advisory system for farmers and gardeners

AI Disease Detection

Advanced deep learning model with 99.66% accuracy trained on 38 disease classes across 14 plant types.

Treatment Recommendations

Comprehensive fertilizer and organic manure recommendations tailored to specific diseases and plant types.

Weather Integration

Real-time weather analysis to assess disease risk based on environmental conditions.
